01 / About

Designing AI for the real clinical world.

I build end-to-end AI systems that turn messy biomedical data into trustworthy tools for clinicians and researchers — from fine-tuning transformers on 3M+ EHR notes to deploying LLM-powered RAG pipelines in production.

My work sits at the intersection of natural language processing, large language models, and medical informatics. I've led projects across the University of Iowa, NIH / NCATS, and the California Medical Innovation Institute — with a strong focus on rigor, reproducibility, and real-world clinical impact.
